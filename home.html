Why do we eat food?

Food provides the energy that our bodies need to keep going. Without it, we would not be able to survive. However, food is not just requriement in our lives. For many people, food has more meaning than that as the look, touch and smell and often be assoicated to memories and bring forward certain feelings!

We all must have a certain dish that when we think of instantly makes us happy! For some that may be spaghetti bolognese, for some that could be chicken kebabs or some it could even be beans on toast.
