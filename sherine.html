Biryani
  • Alex's favourite food
What is it?

It is a dish made with rice, spices, and either meat or vegetables. All the ingredients are usually cooked all together and it usually takes 2 or 3 hours so it is usually made for special occasions!

Origins

Various countires in South Asia such as India, Pakistan and Bangladesh

Ingredients needed to make it (serves 5):
Boiled basmati rice
salt as required
2 teaspoon virgin olive oil
3 green cardamom
2 clove
2 onion
1 teaspoon turmeric
1 teaspoon turmeric
1 tablespoon garlic paste
600 gm chicken
1 tablespoon garlic paste
1 teaspoon red chilli powder
1 tablespoon garam masala powder
Where can you try this?

Going to local indian resturants would be the most convenient way for you try some biryani but the most authentic experice would be to get your Indian mate's mum to make some!
